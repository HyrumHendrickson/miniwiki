Music

Music
ScopeThe art and science of organized sound
Major topicsMusic Theory, Classical Music, Musical Instruments, Folk Music, Counterpoint
DisciplinesMusicology, music theory, ethnomusicology, performance practice

Music is the art of organizing sound in time to create aesthetic and expressive experiences. One of the oldest and most universal of human activities, music is present in every known culture and plays roles ranging from religious ritual and collective celebration to personal expression and emotional communication. The study of music encompasses both its technical dimensions — the theory of harmony, rhythm, and form — and its historical, cultural, and social contexts, as explored by musicologists, ethnomusicologists, music theorists, and performers.
