Natural Sciences

Natural Sciences
ScopeEmpirical study of the physical world
Major disciplinesPhysics, Chemistry, Astronomy, Geology, Thermodynamics
MethodObservation, experimentation, mathematical modeling

The natural sciences are the disciplines that investigate the physical world through systematic observation, experimentation, and mathematical description. From the subatomic particles described by quantum mechanics to the vast structures of the cosmos studied by astronomy, the natural sciences have produced humanity's most reliable and powerful knowledge of nature.
