Mathematics is the discipline of pure reason. From Euclid's Elements to Newton's calculus, the Western mathematical tradition has sought not merely to calculate but to understand — to grasp why results are true, and to perceive the deep structure of quantity, space, and change. Articles here emphasize conceptual clarity, use LaTeX for precise notation, and include interactive Desmos graphs wherever they illuminate the ideas.
